About Me

Peter Amezquita has more than 10 years of accounting experience. He has worked with clients of all sizes, from startup companies to the Fortune 100. Peter has specific experience in Federal taxation, state taxation, business analysis, and financial reporting. Industries that he has worked with include: pharmaceutical, retail, distribution, and lighting.

Peter has an active CPA License in the state of New Jersey and is currently the Controller for a Food Distibutor and a part time CFO for an LED Lighting Contractor. Not only is he a great accountant, but he is also a skilled Web Developer. He specializes in building business applications and automating accounting job functions.
